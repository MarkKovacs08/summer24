Jujutsu Kaisen Summary

Plot

Jujutsu Kaisen is about sourcerers that used cursed energy to defeat cursed spirits like how exterminators
are respondible for getting rid of bugs and pests. Our story starts of woth a non-sourcerer named yuji eating a
finger belonging to the king of curses Sukuna. This gives Yuji the ability to used cursed energy however he could
turn into Sukuna if he isnt carefull, after learning how dangerous Sukuna is Yuji chooses to go on a journey and
consume all of Sukuna's fingers so when he dies so does the biggest threat to the world.

A couple of the main charecters

Yuji Itadori

Yuji is the official main charecter of the show he is natuarlly very strong and fast so his main
goal of the show is to increase his ability to control his cursed energy. With the help of his friends
he strives to achive his goal of saving peoples lives and making friends so when he dies he wont have
to be alone but instead being surronded people that love him.

Satoru Gojo

Known as the strongest cursed user of today he is a teacher at Jujutsu High a school dedicated to
taking in cursed users an making them into trained jujutsu sourcerers. He looks over the first year
students that come to Jujutsu High. He has a very cocky personality since his skill is unable to be
challenged by many people in the world.

Megumi Fushiguro

A first year at Jujutsu High Megumi was taken in by Gojo at a young age to be a sourcerer. Being
taught by one of the greatest sourcerer of all time has definitly paid off because he is very powerful
however he doesnt have the same cockiness that gojo has instead he is very quiet and barely shows emotion.

Nobara Kugisaki

Nobara is also a first year in Jujutsu High but unlike Megumi she wasnt raised in a lifestyle where
sorcerers were everywhere. However she is still able to hold her own in a fight because she has a spirit
that wont quit. She is a big fan of fashion and she has a very narrow mindset on everything meaning once
she thinks of something the only thing that would stop her is death.
